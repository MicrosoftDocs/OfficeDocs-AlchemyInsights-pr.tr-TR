1. <span data-ttu-id="42320-103">Yönetim merkezinde [Abonelikler](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?linkid=842054) sayfasına gidin veya **Faturalama** \> **Abonelikler**’i seçin.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="42320-103">In the Admin center, go to the [Subscriptions](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?linkid=842054) page, or choose **Billing** \> **Subscriptions**.</span></span>
